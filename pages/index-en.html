Welcome to Excalibur!

Tampere academic roleplaying club Excalibur RY is a student club focusing on roleplaying-, card-, miniature- and boardgames. Our club room is located in room RD102, on the first floor of Rakennustalo, right next to the main entrance.

Regular Events

The club hosts weekly board-, MtG-, and miniature- games every week, and a TTRPG day once per month! There's even a drink cooler.

Member Benefits

Club members can reserve the room for TTRPGs or board game nights for their friends! They can also rent out boardgames.
